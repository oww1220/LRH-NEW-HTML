Tips for Driving in Jeju

  • Speed Camera

    Speed Cameras are all over the road in Korea. GPS system will announce
    when there is an upcoming speed camera. Be aware of them!

  • Left Turn Signal

    Drivers must wait for left turn signal at the traffic light. An unprotected left
    turn is allowed only when there is sufficient clearance between you and
    the opposing traffic.

  • Roundabouts

    In Jeju, there are many roundabouts on the road. Drivers must yield to others
    already on a roundabout before entry. Some roundabouts has more exits
    than 4, please be aware the right direction.

  • Fogs (especially at night)

    Jeju is an island located at South of mainland Korea. It means the
    environment of Jeju is humid and warm which many fog is caused.
    When fog gets heavy, the maximum visible distance gets closer than few
    meters. Do not forget to drive slowly and use fog lights in foggy weather

  • School Zone

    If you see the School Zone sign above, please drive slowly under 30km/h and
    pay attention. It means there is a school nearby and students may jump into
    the road.

  • Parking

    Most of the time, street parking is not allowed unless there is parking sopt
    marked by white line. If there are numbers on or beside parking sopt,
    it is for LOCAL RESIDENTS ONLY

  • Fine

    Fine for violation of traffic law in Korea will be carged to LOTTE Rental.
    LOTTE Rental will charge additional fee to customers after their use of
    rental cars.
